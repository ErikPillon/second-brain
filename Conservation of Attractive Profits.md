# Law of Conservation of Attractive Profits

When a good, a service or a product gets commoditized (or modularized), the value does not disappear but move to the adjacent step on the value chain. 

Like in the advent of AI with powerful LLM, information is not anymore a scarse resource but becomes widely available and only one click away. This is a classic transition from [Scarsity Economics](<Scarcity Economics.md>) to [Abundancy Economics](<Abundancy Economics.md>)
